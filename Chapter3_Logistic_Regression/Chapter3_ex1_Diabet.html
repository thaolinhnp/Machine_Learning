Thng tin cc ct d liu

  1. Pregnancies: s ln mang thai
  2. Glucose: Nng glucose huyt tng 2 gi trong th nghim dung np glucose ng ung
  3. BloodPressure: Huyt p tm trng (mm Hg)
  4. SkinThickness: dy da gp Triceps skin fold thickness (mm)
  5. Insulin: 2-Hour serum insulin (mu U/ml). insulin huyt thanh 2-gi
  6. BMI: (weight in kg/(height in m)^2)
  7. DiabetesPedigreeFunction: Diabetes pedigree function
  8. Age: Age (years)
  9. Outcome: Class variable (0 or 1) ##### Ch : Tt c cc bin trn lin tc, mc ch l d on ai c b tiu ng hay khng (Outcome=1) da trn cc bin khc. Cc mu ly t ph n trn 21 years old.
